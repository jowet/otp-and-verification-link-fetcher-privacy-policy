OTP Extractor

A Chrome extension that helps you extract OTP codes and verification links from Gmail messages.

Key Features:

  • Automatically extracts OTP codes from incoming emails
  • Finds verification links in your Gmail messages
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with Google
  • All data processed locally on your device
  • No data sent to external servers
View Privacy Policy

This extension is designed with privacy in mind. All data processing happens locally on your device.
